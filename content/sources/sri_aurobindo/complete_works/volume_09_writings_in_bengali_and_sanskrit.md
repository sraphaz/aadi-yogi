# Writings in Bengali and Sanskrit (The Complete Works of Sri Aurobindo Vol. 9)

## About This Volume

All writings in Bengali and Sanskrit, mostly written in 1909 and 1910 for the Calcutta weekly Dharma, including Tales of Prison Life. The Sanskrit writings and some Bengali material were first published posthumously.

## Access

The official PDF is typeset with non-Unicode Bengali fonts, so machine text extraction produces unusable glyph soup. A faithful digitization of this volume needs a dedicated OCR pass with Bengali script support. For personal study, download the official PDF from [the Ashram library](https://www.sriaurobindoashram.org/sriaurobindo/downloadpdf.php?id=25) with `python scripts/ingest/download_ashram_pdfs.py` and convert it locally with `python scripts/convert/pdf_to_markdown.py --include-restricted` (output stays in the gitignored `data/` tree).
