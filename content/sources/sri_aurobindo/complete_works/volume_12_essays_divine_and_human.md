# Essays Divine and Human (The Complete Works of Sri Aurobindo Vol. 12)

## About This Volume

Short prose pieces written between 1910 and 1950 but not published during Sri Aurobindo's lifetime, including Thoughts and Aphorisms. Posthumously published; still under active copyright of the Sri Aurobindo Ashram Trust.

## Access

This work remains under active copyright of the Sri Aurobindo Ashram Trust, so its full text is not stored in this public repository (see `docs/copyright_policy.md`). For personal study, download the official PDF from [the Ashram library](https://www.sriaurobindoashram.org/sriaurobindo/downloadpdf.php?id=27) with `python scripts/ingest/download_ashram_pdfs.py` and convert it locally with `python scripts/convert/pdf_to_markdown.py --include-restricted` (output stays in the gitignored `data/` tree).
