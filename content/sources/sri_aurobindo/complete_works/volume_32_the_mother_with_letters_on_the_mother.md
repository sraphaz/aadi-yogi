# The Mother with Letters on the Mother (The Complete Works of Sri Aurobindo Vol. 32)

## About This Volume

Sri Aurobindo's book The Mother (1928, itself public domain) together with letters on the Mother written in the 1930s and posthumously compiled. The letters remain under active copyright of the Sri Aurobindo Ashram Trust, so only this metadata record is committed.

## Access

This work remains under active copyright of the Sri Aurobindo Ashram Trust, so its full text is not stored in this public repository (see `docs/copyright_policy.md`). For personal study, download the official PDF from [the Ashram library](https://www.sriaurobindoashram.org/sriaurobindo/downloadpdf.php?id=42) with `python scripts/ingest/download_ashram_pdfs.py` and convert it locally with `python scripts/convert/pdf_to_markdown.py --include-restricted` (output stays in the gitignored `data/` tree).
