# Letters on Yoga - IV (The Complete Works of Sri Aurobindo Vol. 31)

## About This Volume

Letters on the integral yoga. Posthumously compiled; still under active copyright of the Sri Aurobindo Ashram Trust.

## Access

This work remains under active copyright of the Sri Aurobindo Ashram Trust, so its full text is not stored in this public repository (see `docs/copyright_policy.md`). For personal study, download the official PDF from [the Ashram library](https://www.sriaurobindoashram.org/sriaurobindo/downloadpdf.php?id=70) with `python scripts/ingest/download_ashram_pdfs.py` and convert it locally with `python scripts/convert/pdf_to_markdown.py --include-restricted` (output stays in the gitignored `data/` tree).
