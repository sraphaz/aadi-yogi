# Rig Veda - Mandala 10 Hymn 185

## Public-Domain Translation

1. GREAT, unassailable must he the heavenly favour of' Three Gods, Varuna, Mitra, Aryaman. 2. O'er these, neither at home nor yet abroad or pathways that are Strange, The evil-minded foe hath power 3. Nor over him, the man on whom the Sons of Aditi bestow Eternal light that he may live.
