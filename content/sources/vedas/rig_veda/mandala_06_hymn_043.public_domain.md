# Rig Veda - Mandala 6 Hymn 43

## Public-Domain Translation

1. IN whose wild joy thou madest once Sambara Divodasa's prey, This Soma is pressed out for thee, O Indra: drink! 2. Whose gladdening draught, shed from the points, thou guardest in the midst and end, This Soma is pressed out for thee, O Indra drink! 3. In whose wild joy thou settest free the kine held fast within the rock, This Soma is pressed out for thee, O Indra: drink! 4. This, in whose juice delighting thou gainest the might of Maghavan, This Soma is pressed out for thee, O Indra drink!
