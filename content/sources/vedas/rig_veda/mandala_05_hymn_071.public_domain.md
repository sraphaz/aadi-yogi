# Rig Veda - Mandala 5 Hymn 71

## Public-Domain Translation

1. O Varuna and Mitra, ye who slay the foemen, come with might To this our goodly sacrifice. 2. For, Varuna and Mitra, ye Sages are Rulers over all. Fill full our songs, for this ye can. 3. Come to the juice that we have pressed. Varuna, Mitra, come to drink This Soma of the worshipper.
