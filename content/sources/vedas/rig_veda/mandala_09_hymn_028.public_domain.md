# Rig Veda - Mandala 9 Hymn 28

## Public-Domain Translation

1. URGED by the men, this vigorous Steed, Lord of the mind, Omniscient, Runs to the woolen straining-cloth. 2. Within the filter hath he flowed, this Soma for the Gods effused, Entering all their essences. 3. He shines in beauty there, this God Immortal in his dwelling-place, Foe-slayer, dearest to the Gods. 4. Directed by the Sisters ten, bellowing on his way this Steer Runs onward to the wooden vats. 5. This Pavamana, swift and strong, Omniscient, gave splendour to The Sun and all his forms of light. 6. This Soma being purified, flows mighty and infallible, Slayer of sinners, dear to Gods.
