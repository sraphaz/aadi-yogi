# Rig Veda - Mandala 10 Hymn 175

## Public-Domain Translation

1. MAY Savitar the God, O Stones, stir you according to the Law: Be harnessed to the shafts, and press. 2. Stones, drive calamity away, drive ye away malevolence: Make ye the Cows our medicine. 3. Of one accord the upper Stones, giving the Bull his bull-like strength, Look down with pride on those below. 4. May Savitar the God, O Stones, stir you as Law commands for him Who sacrifices, pouring juice.
