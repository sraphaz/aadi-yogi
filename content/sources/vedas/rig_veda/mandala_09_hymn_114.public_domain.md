# Rig Veda - Mandala 9 Hymn 114

## Public-Domain Translation

1. THE man who walketh as the Laws of Indu Pavamana bid, Men call him rich in children, him, O Soma, who hath met thy thought. Flow, Indu, flow for Indra's sake. 2. Kasyapa, Rsi, lifting up thy voice with hymn-composers' lauds, Pav reverence to King Soma born the Sovran Ruler of the plants. Flow, Indu, flow for Indra's sake. 3. Seven regions have their several Suns; the ministering priests are seven; Seven are the Aditya Deities,-with these, O Soma, guard thou us. Flow, Indu, flow for Indra's sake. 4. Guard us with this oblation which, King Soma, hath been dressed for thee.
