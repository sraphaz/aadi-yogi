# Garuda Purana - Opening Witness

## Public-Domain Translation

Om! Adoration to Vishnu, the destroyer of the universe, the unchangeable, the supreme soul, the cause of creation, preservation, and destruction.

The Garuda Purana is a Puranic dialogue tradition associated with Vishnu and Garuda, often consulted for themes of death, afterlife, ethical consequence, and liberation.

This repository entry preserves an opening witness sample to anchor future provenance-first expansion of the Garuda Purana corpus.

## Notes

Handle afterlife and death themes with care. Do not use Puranic imagery to frighten, coerce, or bypass qualified human support in crisis.
