# Puranas - Canon Intake Index

## Canon Assumption

For initial corpus coverage, `all Puranas` means the 18 Mahapuranas. Upapuranas remain important, but they are treated as a later expansion because the corpus boundary is much less stable.

## Current Coverage

- Imported public-domain text: Vishnu Purana starter chapter.
- Catalogued intake stubs: the remaining Mahapuranas.
- Known public-domain lead already identified: Garuda Purana at the Internet Sacred Text Archive.
