# The Mother

Works by The Mother, handled with particular care around permissions and excerpt boundaries.

- `collected_works/`: metadata records for the Collected Works of the Mother (CWM) from the official Ashram library, with a local-only full-text pipeline (her works remain under active copyright until at least 2034).
- `prayers_and_meditations/`: earlier template scaffolding for curated, permission-cleared selections.
