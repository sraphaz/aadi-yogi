# On Education (Collected Works of the Mother Vol. 12)

## About This Volume

Articles, messages, letters and conversations with students and teachers of the Ashram school, and three plays: Towards the Future, The Great Secret and The Ascent to Truth.

## Access

This work remains under active copyright of the Sri Aurobindo Ashram Trust, so its full text is not stored in this public repository (see `docs/copyright_policy.md`). For personal study, download the official PDF from [the Ashram library](https://www.sriaurobindoashram.org/mother/downloadpdf.php?id=60) with `python scripts/ingest/download_ashram_pdfs.py` and convert it locally with `python scripts/convert/pdf_to_markdown.py --include-restricted` (output stays in the gitignored `data/` tree).
