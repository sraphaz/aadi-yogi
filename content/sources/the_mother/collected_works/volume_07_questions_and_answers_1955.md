# Questions and Answers 1955 (Collected Works of the Mother Vol. 7)

## About This Volume

Answers to questions of Ashram children and disciples, with commentaries on Sri Aurobindo's Bases of Yoga, The Human Cycle and The Synthesis of Yoga, and on her play The Great Secret.

## Access

This work remains under active copyright of the Sri Aurobindo Ashram Trust, so its full text is not stored in this public repository (see `docs/copyright_policy.md`). For personal study, download the official PDF from [the Ashram library](https://www.sriaurobindoashram.org/mother/downloadpdf.php?id=55) with `python scripts/ingest/download_ashram_pdfs.py` and convert it locally with `python scripts/convert/pdf_to_markdown.py --include-restricted` (output stays in the gitignored `data/` tree).
