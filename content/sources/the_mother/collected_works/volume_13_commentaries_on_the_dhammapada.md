# Commentaries on the Dhammapada (Collected Works of the Mother Vol. 13)

## About This Volume

The Mother's commentaries on the Dhammapada, with the text translated by a disciple. (In the English Collected Works this material appears with Questions and Answers, volume 3.)

## Access

This work remains under active copyright of the Sri Aurobindo Ashram Trust, so its full text is not stored in this public repository (see `docs/copyright_policy.md`). For personal study, download the official PDF from [the Ashram library](https://www.sriaurobindoashram.org/mother/downloadpdf.php?id=61) with `python scripts/ingest/download_ashram_pdfs.py` and convert it locally with `python scripts/convert/pdf_to_markdown.py --include-restricted` (output stays in the gitignored `data/` tree).
