# Words of Long Ago (Collected Works of the Mother Vol. 2)

## About This Volume

All writings of the Mother dating from before 1920 (except Prayers and Meditations), including talks given in Paris to small groups of seekers, texts written in Japan, and tales written for children.

## Access

This work remains under active copyright of the Sri Aurobindo Ashram Trust, so its full text is not stored in this public repository (see `docs/copyright_policy.md`). For personal study, download the official PDF from [the Ashram library](https://www.sriaurobindoashram.org/mother/downloadpdf.php?id=50) with `python scripts/ingest/download_ashram_pdfs.py` and convert it locally with `python scripts/convert/pdf_to_markdown.py --include-restricted` (output stays in the gitignored `data/` tree).
