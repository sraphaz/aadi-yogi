# Questions and Answers 1956 (Collected Works of the Mother Vol. 8)

## About This Volume

Answers to questions of Ashram children and disciples, with commentaries on Sri Aurobindo's The Synthesis of Yoga and Thoughts and Glimpses. The year of the supramental manifestation of 29 February 1956.

## Access

This work remains under active copyright of the Sri Aurobindo Ashram Trust, so its full text is not stored in this public repository (see `docs/copyright_policy.md`). For personal study, download the official PDF from [the Ashram library](https://www.sriaurobindoashram.org/mother/downloadpdf.php?id=56) with `python scripts/ingest/download_ashram_pdfs.py` and convert it locally with `python scripts/convert/pdf_to_markdown.py --include-restricted` (output stays in the gitignored `data/` tree).
