# Om and Consciousness States

A comparative synthesis of Om and the four states in the Mandukya Upanishad, linked to ontology and living maps.

## Core Teaching

Om maps the waking, dreaming, deep sleep, and turiya dimensions of experience. Turiya is not another state among states but the witness-ground of all.
