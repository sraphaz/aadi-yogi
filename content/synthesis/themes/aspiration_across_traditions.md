# Aspiration Across Traditions

This synthesis note connects recurring aspiration themes across Vedic invocation, Upanishadic inquiry, Gita action, and Integral Yoga orientation.

## Shared Movement

- Turn from mere habit toward conscious orientation.
- Let aspiration precede technique.
- Keep humility before the greater Reality.

## Source Anchors

- Rig Veda Mandala 1 Hymn 1 — Agni as mediator of offering and aspiration.
- Isha Upanishad — action in the world without possessive clinging.
- Bhagavad Gita Chapter 2 — steadiness beyond grief through Self-knowledge.
- Consciousness Core — `content/consciousness_core/essence.md`

## Cautions

- Do not convert aspiration into pressure or self-contempt.
- Do not treat one tradition's language as the only valid form of sincerity.

## Agent Use

Use when the seeker asks how to begin, renew, or clarify spiritual orientation without needing sectarian proof-texting.
