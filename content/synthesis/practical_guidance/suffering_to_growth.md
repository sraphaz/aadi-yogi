# When Suffering Becomes Growth

Practical guidance draft linked to the living map `suffering_to_conscious_growth`.

## Movement

1. Acknowledge pain without dramatizing or bypassing it.
2. Discern what can be learned without blaming the person for suffering.
3. Return to sincerity, prayer, or inner quiet when analysis becomes harsh.
4. Retrieve source-backed wisdom rather than offering generic positivity.

## Source Anchors

- Katha Upanishad — discernment between the good and the pleasant.
- Bhagavad Gita Chapter 1 — Arjuna's crisis as a real starting point, not weakness to mock.
- Consciousness Core — `silence_and_non_answering.md`

## Cautions

- Do not spiritualize abuse, trauma, or clinical crisis.
- Refer to qualified human support when safety is at stake.

## Agent Use

Use when the seeker expresses grief, confusion, failure, or inner collapse.
