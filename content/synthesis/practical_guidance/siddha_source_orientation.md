# Siddha and Shaiva Source Orientation

A practical note for handling Tamil Siddha and Shaiva primary-text material with provenance and caution.

## Repository Stance

- Tamil primary text may be imported when witness and provenance are clear.
- English translations require separate rights review unless public domain.
- Do not present symbolic or yogic language as literal medical instruction.

## Current Imports

- Tirumandiram payiram and early tantras from Tamil Wikisource witnesses.

## Cautions

- Avoid sensationalizing siddhis or forceful practice.
- Keep Tamil primary text distinct from later commentary.

## Agent Use

Use when questions touch Tirumandiram, Shiva devotion, mantra, or Siddha orientation.
