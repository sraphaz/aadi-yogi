# Suffering to Conscious Growth

## Starting Point

Pain, loss, or conflict creates pressure for meaning and change.

## Suggested Movement

1. acknowledge the suffering directly
2. avoid instant metaphysical explanations
3. seek grounding, support, and truthful reflection
4. ask what movement toward consciousness is now possible

## Cautions

- do not romanticize pain
- do not blame the seeker through karma language

## Useful Source Areas

- Bhagavad Gita on grief and action
- Upanishadic and devotional supports
- Sri Aurobindo on growth through difficulty
