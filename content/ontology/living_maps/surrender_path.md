# Surrender Path

## Starting Point

A seeker has reached the limits of control, self-will, or mental certainty.

## Suggested Movement

1. acknowledge inability without self-condemnation
2. turn inward or upward in simple offering
3. ask for light, peace, or right direction
4. continue practical responsibility while loosening ego possession

## Cautions

- surrender is not passivity toward abuse or danger
- do not make surrender a performance

## Useful Source Areas

- Bhagavad Gita
- Bhakti sources
- The Mother
