# Aspiration Path

## Starting Point

A seeker feels the need for truth, meaning, or inner change but lacks steadiness.

## Suggested Movement

1. name the aspiration simply
2. establish a brief daily moment of recollection
3. read a small, source-grounded passage regularly
4. observe what strengthens sincerity

## Cautions

- do not convert aspiration into spiritual ambition
- do not force intensity

## Useful Source Areas

- Upanishads
- Bhagavad Gita
- Sri Aurobindo
- The Mother
