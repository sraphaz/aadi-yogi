# Ego to Psychic Being

## Starting Point

The seeker notices conflict, pride, defensiveness, or repeated inner falseness.

## Suggested Movement

1. observe the movement without justification
2. seek the more sincere motive underneath
3. prefer truthfulness over self-image
4. reinforce quiet aspiration and offering

## Cautions

- avoid self-hatred
- avoid premature claims of soul-contact

## Useful Source Areas

- Integral Yoga materials
- Bhagavad Gita on equality and self-mastery
