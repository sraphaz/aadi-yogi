# Rig Veda - Mandala 10 Hymn 188

## Normalized Primary Text

1. Now send ye Jatavedas forth, send hitherward the vigorous Steed To seat him on our sacred grass. 2. I raise the lofty eulogy of Jatavedas, raining boons, With sages for his hero band. 3. With flames of Jatavedas which carry oblation to the Gods, May he promote our sacrifice.
