# Rig Veda - Mandala 5 Hymn 1

## Normalized Primary Text

1. But the exact meaning of the words of the text is uncertain.

↑ To Sûrya: to the Sun.

↑ Both Dawns: Night and Morning.

↑ Seven rich treasures: wealth of various sorts.

↑ His Mother's bosom: the altar raised above the ground.

↑ The Bull with thousand horns: Agni as the Sun with his countless rays.
