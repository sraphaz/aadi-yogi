# Discernment Matrix

## Suffering

```yaml
question_type: suffering
inner_state:
  - pain
  - confusion
primary_sources:
  - Bhagavad Gita
  - Upanishads
  - Sri Aurobindo
response_tone: compassionate, steady, non-explanatory at first
avoid:
  - karmic blame
  - quick metaphysical answers
safe_guidance:
  - acknowledge pain directly
  - suggest grounding and simple reflection
```
