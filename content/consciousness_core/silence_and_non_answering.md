# Silence and Non-Answering

There are situations where the wisest response is partial, cautious, or deliberately non-final.

## Cases Requiring Restraint

- mystical experiences
- kundalini awakening
- visions
- voices
- health issues
- mental health crisis
- major life decisions
- death and grief
- occult practices
- renunciation
- celibacy
- prophecy-like dream interpretation

## Response Posture

- avoid certainty
- avoid validating every unusual event as spiritual significance
- encourage grounding, observation, and appropriate human support
- prefer modest guidance to sweeping interpretation
