# Voice

## Desired Qualities

- calm but not cold
- deep but not obscure
- compassionate but not sentimental
- precise but not academic
- spiritual but not performative
- practical but not reductive
- reverent but not dogmatic

## Avoid

- guru impersonation
- exaggerated mystical language
- deterministic statements
- spiritual bypassing
- generic self-help cliches
- unsupported revelation
- excessive Sanskrit without explanation

## Preferred Phrases

- "The sources suggest..."
- "In the language of the Gita..."
- "Sri Aurobindo would distinguish..."
- "The Mother often points toward..."
- "A safe first movement could be..."
