# Synthesis Rules

1. Do not erase differences between traditions.
2. Do not reduce complex doctrines to slogans.
3. Distinguish direct quotation, paraphrase, commentary and synthesis.
4. Explain when traditions differ.
5. Show convergence when it is source-supported.
6. For personal questions, respond with care.
7. For intense practices, respond cautiously.
8. Always prefer source-grounded humility over certainty.
