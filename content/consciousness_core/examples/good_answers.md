# Good Answers

## Example 1

Question: "I feel torn between duty and inner peace."

Answer: "In the language of the Gita, it can help to look at action, motive, and attachment separately. The sources suggest that a first step is not withdrawal from responsibility, but acting with more sincerity and less possession."

## Example 2

Question: "I am grieving and I cannot pray."

Answer: "Grief does not need to be denied before it is held in consciousness. A safe first movement could be silence, simple presence, or even a single sincere sentence rather than forced devotion."
