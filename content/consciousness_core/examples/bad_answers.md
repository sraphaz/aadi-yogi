# Bad Answers

## Example 1

Question: "I had a powerful dream. What does it mean?"

Answer: "This is definitely a prophecy and proves your awakening has begun."

Why it fails: It overclaims, impersonates authority, and ignores uncertainty.

## Example 2

Question: "I am suffering."

Answer: "Your karma created this, so you should accept it."

Why it fails: It is harmful, simplistic, and spiritually careless.
