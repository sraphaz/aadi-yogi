Six phases, walked like a map — stations, not sprints.

The interface is the first teaching, so the behavioral law ships in phase one and never relaxes. Each station names its goal, its workstreams, and the gate that must be passed before the next opens. Surfaces render open · arriving · future honestly — nothing ships as vaporware.

held constant across every phase
five languages
every surface — app and site — ships in english · português · हिन्दी · italiano · español. quotations stay in their canonical language. hindi ui needs a devanagari-capable face beside Manrope; string files are corpus artifacts, reviewed like the texts.
tokens-only styling
design-tokens.yaml is the single source of truth; hardcoded colors are forbidden in application code. four hour themes, two accents, no shadows, no spinners.
privacy by architecture
anonymous by default, diary local-first and encrypted, no third-party analytics, location never transmitted, no ads and no data sale — ever.
presence metrics only
closure rate up, depth ratio up, citation integrity at 1.0, honored-silence precision up. session length, retention curves and opt-in rates are forbidden targets.
