Work done as offering ceases to bind. The Gita does not ask you to abandon action —
Thou hast a right to action, but only to action, never to its fruits.
Bhagavad Gita, II.47 · gita
the traditions differ here
Classical Vedanta counsels renunciation of action; the Gita and the Mother teach renunciation in action — consecration, not withdrawal.
