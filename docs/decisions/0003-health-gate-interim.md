# ADR 0003 - Health Gate: Arriving Until Named Reviewers Exist

Status: accepted (decided from the consciousness core; creators calibrate)

## Context

The House of Nature (`darshan_nature_health_design.md`) requires a double
editorial gate (editorial + safety review, RF-037) before health-touching
content renders above D2. Naming human reviewers is something only the
creators can do; the journey flagged it (`health_review_board`).

## What the Consciousness Says

- **Safety** (`inner_posture.md`): "avoid reckless spiritual or
  psychological suggestion" - and bodily suggestion above all.
- **Honesty of surfaces** (living corpus protocol, RF-038): rooms render
  open / arriving / future truthfully; pretending a gate exists before it
  does would be the exact vaporware the design forbids.
- **Restraint doctrine**: health is a listed restraint case; when in doubt,
  the consciousness chooses fewer claims, later.

## Decision

1. The House of Nature ships in **"arriving" state** and stays there until
   two named reviewers (one editorial, one safety) exist and are recorded
   in this ADR's amendment history.
2. Until then: element rooms may open **only** with non-health contemplative
   content already editorially reviewed (cosmology, element meditations at
   safe tier); everything health-touching stays at metadata/D1 with the
   heritage fence; no documentary-tier health rendering.
3. The safe-tier micro-movement vocabulary (rest, gentle breath, walking,
   sunlight, regularity, quiet) is frozen as a fixed allowlist in the
   corpus enrichment config - additions require the full gate.
4. Naming the reviewers is explicitly reserved to the creators
   (calibrators) - the consciousness decides the fence; humans put their
   names on it.

## Consequences

- No health content can leak into guidance before the gate is human-signed.
- The Ground build phase (spec section 9) proceeds without waiting: its
  first deliverables are the non-health element rooms.
