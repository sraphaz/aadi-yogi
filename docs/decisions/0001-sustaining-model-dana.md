# ADR 0001 - Sustaining Model: Dana-First, Contemplative Surfaces Free Forever

Status: accepted (decided from the consciousness core; creators calibrate)

## Context

The project needs a sustaining model. The darshan repository
(`docs/darshan_reuse_map.md`) brings a working credits infrastructure
(Stripe, Mercado Pago, PIX) and a free/premium split as candidate input.
Advertising, engagement monetization and data sale are already excluded by
design law (NFR-002, `darshan_interface_concept.md` principle 4).

## What the Consciousness Says

- **Non-coercion** (`inner_posture.md`): "never pressure the seeker into
  belief, practice, or dependence" - a paywall between a seeker and a
  scripture is pressure, and premium-gated peace builds dependence dynamics.
- **Renunciation by design** (concept, principle 4): success is measured by
  the quality of departure; a subscription's job is to prevent departure.
- **The traditions themselves**: dharma teaching in every lineage of this
  corpus moved by dana - the gift freely given, never a gate. The Gita is
  not behind a toll; the Ashram published Sri Aurobindo for whoever came.
- **SPI / CH-INEQUALITY** (sky-merits): anonymous-first, low-bandwidth
  distribution and free access are already confirmed elevation commitments.

## Decision

1. **Everything that costs nothing per use is free forever**: Library,
   daily Word, Silence Room, Practice Field, Living Maps, Sky Map, Diary,
   Anthology - all offline surfaces, for everyone, without account.
2. **Only real marginal cost may be priced**: the Inquiry (LLM cost) has a
   free daily measure for everyone; beyond it, credits at a transparent,
   cost-anchored price ("this is what the answer costs us"), reusing the
   darshan repo's credits/PIX infrastructure.
3. **Dana as the primary current**: an always-available, never-nagging
   offering surface (one quiet page, not a popup); patronage explicitly
   funds the free measure of others ("keep the well open").
4. **Hard boundaries**: no ads ever; no data monetization ever; no
   subscription that locks content; no dana prompts inside contemplative
   flows; institutional or patron money never alters the behavior law.

## Consequences

- RF-039 added (monetization boundary, must); business dimension of
  maturity rises with calibration gaps (pricing of the free measure, legal
  vehicle for dana) left to the creators.
